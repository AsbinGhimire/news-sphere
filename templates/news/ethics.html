Editorial Ethics Code

Integrity is the bedrock of GlobalDiscovery journalism.

1. Accuracy and Verification

We strive for complete accuracy. Our reporters are required to verify every fact and consult multiple sources before publication.

2. Independence

GlobalDiscovery remains fiercely independent from political parties, corporate interests, and lobbying groups. Our only loyalty is to the truth.

3. AI Ethics

While we use AI to help categorize and synthesize news, all 'Intelligence Insights' are clearly labeled, and final editorial decisions are always made by human journalists.
